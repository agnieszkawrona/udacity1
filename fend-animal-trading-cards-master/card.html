Seal

Seals are group of marine mammals that live in various regions of the world. They can survive both in polar and in tropic water.

  • Scientific Name: Phoca vitulina
  • Type: Mammal
  • Diet: Carnivore
  • Lifestyle: Herd

Seals are divided in two families: one that includes seals with ears, like sea lion, and other that includes earless seals, like common seal. There are 33 species of seals in total
